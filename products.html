Brands We Have

Diamond Paints

Diamond Paints is a trusted name in Pakistan, offering high-quality paints for both interior and exterior surfaces. It provides long-lasting finishes, vibrant colors, and smooth application suitable for homes, offices, and commercial buildings. Their range includes emulsion paints, weathercoat exterior paints, metal paints, wood finishes, primers, undercoats, and wall putty, ensuring complete surface protection and a premium finish.

Sparco Paint

Sparco Paint offers durable, high-performance paints designed to withstand harsh weather conditions. Ideal for exterior walls, fences, and commercial projects, Sparco ensures long-lasting colors and protection. Their products include weathercoat exterior paints, oil paints, roof coatings, wood and metal finishes, primers, undercoats, and wall putty to prepare surfaces perfectly before painting.

Gobi’s Paints

Gobi’s Paints specializes in smooth and durable finishes for wood, metal, and interior surfaces. Known for premium quality, Gobi’s enhances the natural beauty of wood while providing protection. Their products include wood finishes, metal paints, gloss enamels, primers, undercoats, wall putty, and interior emulsions, making them a complete solution for painting and surface preparation.

ICI Dulux

ICI Dulux is an international premium paint brand offering vibrant and long-lasting colors for interior and exterior surfaces. Known for smooth application and excellent durability, their products include Weathershield exterior paints, Velvet Touch interior emulsions, gloss and enamel paints, primers, undercoats, wall putty, and decorative coatings, ensuring professional-quality finishes for any project.

Master Paints

Master Paints is known for its high-quality, affordable paints suitable for both residential and commercial projects. Their range includes interior and exterior emulsions, metal paints, wood finishes, primers, undercoats, and wall putty. Master Paints offers vibrant colors, smooth application, and durable finishes, making it a reliable choice for any painting project.
